Game Manual

1. Overview

Back to the wild is a 3D platformer game set in a zoo in which you start off taking control of a raccoon who decides to rescue the animals within the zoo. The player must solve puzzles and maneuver around different animal's exhibits in order to find stars that unlock the exhibit for the animal to escape from. Escaped animals can then be used by the player in to solve other animal's exhibits.
